35 Native IOS and Android App

Overview

The 35 app is a native android and Ios app designed for lovers and friends of film photography. It allows film photography fanatics to upload and view film photo masterpieces from a specialized community within the app

Objective

The objective of this research is to build Native IOS and Android apps that allow users to upload photos, read blogs and engage in previously uploaded film photography content

Role

UI/UX Designer

Tools

Figma, Photoshop

Competitive Analysis

As the UI designer, I start brainstorming by conducting competitive analysis. The three main apps I looked at were Tumblr, Instagram, and Vsco. These 3 apps allow users to share photos in their own unique way, despite being different they all did have some similarities. They all allowed users to upload/share photos, have a personalized profile, and scroll through a “feed” of some sort

UI Design Rationale

Film photography is an art known to be all about light. Most steps involving film such as inserting film in the camera, and developing film are done in a dark room. This is why I decided that a dark UI was most appropriate. Overall Film photography is all about its natural, raw, unedited form therefore the design is simple in order to make allow the photos to take center stage
